About India

India is a land of incredible diversity . From the snow-capped peaks of the Himalyas to the scene backwaters of Kerela, every corner of India tells a unique story. India is the second-most populous country in the world and boasts a history spanning thousands of years. Its rich traditions have left an incredible mark on the world.



India : A land of cultural and geographical diversity

"Unity in diversity is not just a slogan in India but a way of life"

Highlights of India include:

  • Architecture: Majestic monuments like the Taj Mahal and Red Fort
  • Cuisine: Diverse flavours from spicy curries to sweet deserts.
  • Culture: Vibrant festivals , classical dances and colourful attire.

Delicious Indian Cuisine

India's food is as diverse as its culture . From the fiery curries of the south to the beauty bread of the north , there'ssomething for everyone. Popular dishes include:

  • North Indian: Butter chicken, naan and paneer tikka.
  • South Indian: Dosa,idli and sambhaare
  • Street Food: Pani-puri, chaat and vada-pav

Indian Culture

India's cultural heritage is a blend of various traditions, languages and religions. Each region has its unique identity and traditions that are celebrated thorugh festivals and daily life. Here are some highlights :

  • Classical Dance's: Bharatnatyam,Kathak,Odissi
  • Music: Traditional instruments like sitar,tabla and veena.
  • Festivals: Diwali,Holi,Eid and Christmas bring people together in celebration.
